Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and are committed to giving you full control of your data. If you’ve logged into our platform using your Facebook account, you may request to have your account and all associated data permanently deleted.

How to request deletion:

  1. Send an email to support@advisorcheck.info with the subject line “Data Deletion Request.”
  2. Include your full name and the email address linked to your Facebook account.
  3. Once verified, we will permanently delete your account and all related data within 7 business days.

Please note: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your account and all related posts, logs, and compliance records cannot be recovered.

For any questions, please contact our support team at support@advisorcheck.info.
